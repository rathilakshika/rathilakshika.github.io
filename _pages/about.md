I am a first-year graduate student in the Computer Sciences Department at the University of Wisconsin-Madison. Currently, I am a member of the [Quest Lab](https://quest-lab.cs.wisc.edu/), under the guidance of [Prof. Swamit Tannu](https://swamittannu.com/). My research interests predominantly revolve around the intersection of Quantum Computing and Machine Learning. My current research focusses on understanding the origins of classical parallelism and on how quantum computers, particularly neutral atom-based systems, can leverage those.

I hold a Bachelor's in Electrical Engineering with a minor in Computer Science from the Indian Institute of Technology Delhi. During my undergrad, I focused on various aspects of Quantum Computing, gaining practical experience through internships in research labs and industry.
